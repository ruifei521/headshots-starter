## Head-to-head: five dimensions that matter

### 1. Price and value

**SnapProHead wins on entry price and photos per dollar.** At $29 for 40 HD headshots, you're paying about $0.72 per photo. Aragon's entry tier typically costs more while delivering fewer photos — though exact counts vary by current plan.

For a solo professional who just needs a few polished LinkedIn photos, the price difference matters. If you're outfitting a 50-person company with procurement requirements, Aragon's team features may justify the premium.

### 2. Speed

**SnapProHead is faster on delivery.** Our Flux-based pipeline generates headshots in approximately 25 minutes from training completion. Aragon's turnaround ranges from hours to a full day depending on tier and queue depth.

If you have a profile update due tomorrow morning, speed is a non-negotiable — and SnapProHead wins it.

### 3. Image quality

**This is subjective — and both tools deliver professional results.**

- **SnapProHead** uses Flux AI at 1024×1024 resolution on all paid plans. The output emphasizes natural skin textures, realistic lighting, and consistent framing — designed to look like a studio portrait, not obvious AI art.
- **Aragon AI** delivers 896×1088 on standard tiers and 1792×2176 on Executive. The higher resolution is meaningful if you need print-ready images (magazines, large-format displays). For digital use (LinkedIn, websites, email signatures), both resolutions are more than sufficient.

Real user feedback from Reddit (r/ChatGPT, r/ArtificialIntelligence) generally describes Aragon as "most realistic" and "actually looks like me" — and SnapProHead users report similar satisfaction with likeness and consistency. The honest truth: **both tools can produce excellent results when you upload good selfies.** Bad input photos hurt any AI generator equally.

### 4. Privacy and data handling

**Both tools have published privacy policies — read them before uploading.**

- **SnapProHead** offers privacy-first handling with **delete on request** for uploads and generated images. Your selfies are used only for your headshot generation, not for training shared models. See our [privacy policy](/privacy).
- **Aragon AI** publishes its privacy policy on its website and includes SOC 2 Type II certification — a strong signal for enterprise buyers concerned about compliance.

For an individual user, both privacy postures are reasonable. For a regulated industry (healthcare, legal, finance), Aragon's SOC 2 certification may be required by your compliance team.

### 5. Refund and redo policies

**Both offer recourse if results disappoint.**

- **SnapProHead** provides a Satisfaction Guarantee: if you're unhappy with your first batch, we'll generate a new set with different settings at no cost. Read the full [refund policy](/refund).
- **Aragon AI** offers free redos and a refund policy that applies if photos haven't been downloaded yet. Check their current refund page for details.

Both policies are competitive — neither is a meaningful differentiator unless you expect to need multiple redos.

Yes — both use advanced AI models (SnapProHead uses Flux, Aragon uses proprietary models built by AI researchers). The quality gap between top AI headshot tools narrowed significantly in 2025-2026. Your input selfie quality matters more than the specific tool you choose.

### How do I get the best results from either tool?

Upload **8–15 clear selfies** from different days with even lighting and neutral expressions. Avoid sunglasses, hats, heavy filters, and group shots. Include both shoulders-up and waist-up frames. The AI does the rest.

### Is Aragon AI's 40M headshots claim accurate?

As of 2026, Aragon's public marketing cites "millions of headshots generated." The exact 40M figure comes from their website and PR; we have not independently verified it. What matters more than the count is consistent quality across diverse user inputs — both tools deliver on that.

### Should I choose Aragon if I need SOC 2 compliance?

Yes — if your organization requires SOC 2 Type II certified vendors, Aragon's certification simplifies the procurement conversation. SnapProHead is a young company and has not yet completed SOC 2 certification. For solo professionals, this is rarely a blocker. For enterprise procurement, it can be.

### Which tool is better for a tight deadline?

SnapProHead's 25-minute typical delivery beats Aragon's hours-to-1-day turnaround. If you have a profile update due tomorrow morning or a conference bio needed by end of day, SnapProHead is the safer choice.

### What if I want to try both before deciding?
