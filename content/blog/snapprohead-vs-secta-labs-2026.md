## How we compared them

## Head-to-head: five dimensions that matter

### 1. Price and value

**SnapProHead wins on entry price and photos per dollar.** At $29 for 40 HD headshots, you're paying about $0.72 per photo. Secta Labs' entry tier starts at roughly $49 for 16 photos + 4 retouched — that's roughly $2.45 per delivered photo, and most of them are AI-only.

For a solo professional who just needs a few polished LinkedIn photos, the price difference matters. If you specifically want a human retoucher's eye on selected photos, Secta's premium may be worth it.

### 2. Speed

**SnapProHead is faster on delivery.** Our Flux-based pipeline generates headshots in approximately 25 minutes from training completion. Secta's turnaround includes a human review step, so standard delivery runs 2+ hours.

If you have a profile update due tomorrow morning, speed is a non-negotiable — and SnapProHead wins it. If you can wait a few hours and want retouching, Secta is competitive.

### 3. Image quality

**Both tools deliver professional results — the difference is the human touch.**

- **SnapProHead** uses Flux AI at 1024×1024 resolution on all paid plans. The output emphasizes natural skin textures, realistic lighting, and consistent framing — designed to look like a studio portrait, not obvious AI art.
- **Secta Labs** delivers high-resolution AI output with selected photos retouched by a human editor. The retouched photos get a more "studio-polished" look, but the AI-only photos in the same batch are similar to what you'd get from any leading AI tool.

Real user feedback from Reddit (r/ChatGPT, r/ArtificialIntelligence) generally describes Secta as "polished but pricier" and SnapProHead as "fast and accurate." The honest truth: **both tools can produce excellent results when you upload good selfies.** Bad input photos hurt any AI generator equally.

### 4. Human review / retouching

**Secta Labs includes human retouching; SnapProHead does not.**

This is the **biggest differentiator** between the two tools. Secta's higher tiers include a real person reviewing and polishing a small set of the AI-generated photos. SnapProHead delivers AI output only — no human in the loop.

If you have a specific need (a tiny blemish to remove, a stubborn stray hair, a particular retouching style), Secta's human pass is meaningful. If you want a fast, affordable batch of clean photos, SnapProHead's AI-only output is usually good enough.

### 5. Privacy and data handling

**Both tools have published privacy policies — read them before uploading.**

- **SnapProHead** commits to a 30-day auto-delete policy for generated assets. Your selfies are used only for your headshot generation, not for training shared models.
- **Secta Labs** publishes its privacy policy on its website. The presence of a human reviewer means your selfies may be viewed by a Secta team member — relevant if you have strict privacy preferences.

For an individual user, both privacy postures are reasonable. For a regulated industry (healthcare, legal, finance), evaluate which vendor's posture fits your compliance needs.

## Frequently asked questions

### Is SnapProHead really comparable to Secta Labs in quality?

For AI-only output, yes — both use advanced AI models (SnapProHead uses Flux, Secta uses proprietary models). The quality gap on the AI-only portion is small. The meaningful difference is Secta's human retouching on a subset of delivered photos.

### Why is SnapProHead cheaper than Secta Labs?

SnapProHead operates leaner — we don't include human retouching in our pipeline, which keeps our costs lower and our turnaround faster. That efficiency translates to lower prices and more photos per dollar.

### Should I trust a newer brand over an established one?

Secta Labs' mature review profile is a real advantage — you're not wrong to consider them. SnapProHead's Satisfaction Guarantee is designed to remove that risk: if your results don't meet expectations, you're covered.

### Can I use either tool for my law firm or medical practice website?

Yes. Both tools are used by professionals across industries. Check with your firm's compliance team if you're in a regulated industry.

### How do I get the best results from either tool?

Upload **8–15 clear selfies** from different days with even lighting and neutral expressions. Avoid sunglasses, hats, heavy filters, and group shots. Include both shoulders-up and waist-up frames. The AI does the rest.

### What if I want both speed AND retouching?

Generate with SnapProHead for speed and volume, then send your 2–3 favorites to a third-party retoucher for an additional polish pass. This hybrid approach is often cheaper than Secta's retouching tiers and gives you full control over the final look.

### Is human retouching really necessary?

For most LinkedIn, website, and directory use cases, no. Modern AI tools produce output indistinguishable from a studio portrait at typical viewing sizes. Human retouching matters more for print, billboards, and high-stakes executive portraits.
